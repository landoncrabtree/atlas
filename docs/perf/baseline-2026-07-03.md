## `atlas_fs::view_model` (open → fully loaded)

| Bench | Median | 95 % CI |
|---|---:|---|
| `open_and_load_1000` | 35.03 ms | [34.79, 35.28] |
| `open_and_load_5000` | 245.53 ms | [242.17, 248.94] |
| `open_and_load_10000` | 729.07 ms | [723.10, 735.15] |

Ratio 10k / 1k ≈ **20.8×** for a 10× data increase → clear
super-linear (quadratic-shaped) behaviour. The dominant term is
`Inner::recompute` running once per `ListEvent::Batch(64)`, so the
loader does ≈ N / 64 filter-clone-and-sort passes over an ever-growing
raw vec. Address in a follow-up optimization pass.

## `atlas_search::fuzzy`

| Bench | Median | 95 % CI |
|---|---:|---|
| `rank_2k_candidates_ctrl` | 4.48 ms | [4.38, 4.56] |
| `rank_2k_candidates_walker` | 4.32 ms | [4.28, 4.35] |
| `rank_10k_candidates_shell` | 22.04 ms | [21.87, 22.17] |

`fuzzy_rank` builds a fresh `nucleo::Matcher` per candidate today
(`fuzzy_score` allocates + parses on every call). Expected win from
matcher reuse: 3–5× on 10k candidates.

## `atlas_ops::copy`

| Bench | Median | 95 % CI |
|---|---:|---|
| `copy_100_files_4kb` | 15.65 ms | [14.72, 16.42] |
| `copy_1_file_16mb` | 4.04 ms | [3.92, 4.24] |

Small-file copy is dominated by per-item mutex locks + PathBuf clones
in `add_bytes_progress` and friends; the 16 MiB copy is dominated by
the 16× 1 MiB read/write loop (16 PathBuf clones per file).
